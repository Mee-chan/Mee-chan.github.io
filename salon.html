David Salon: Online Booking System

Web

David's Salon faces a critical issue due to their outdated booking system, causing inconvenience for customers and resulting in overcrowding at specific times. This leads to customer dissatisfaction, long waiting times, potential loss of customers, negative reviews, and ultimately, a decline in sales. The proposed solution is a user-friendly web app that allows customers to schedule appointments on specific dates and times. Customers can input their details, choose a desired hairstyle from available options, and efficiently book their slots. This streamlined system ensures a smooth and organized experience, preventing overcrowding, reducing wait times, enhancing customer satisfaction, and safeguarding the salon's reputation.
